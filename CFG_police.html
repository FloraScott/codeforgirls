Police

Going to the police may seem daunting, however if you know what information they need from a testimony it is much simpler. It is the only way to get a legal injunction against an attacker, such as a restraining order, and the only way to ensure that they don't reoffend. Your college nurse and the University counselling service will give you the information and support you need if you decide to take it to the police.
